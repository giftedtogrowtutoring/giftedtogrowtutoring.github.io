## 3. Approach — `/approach`

**Goal:** Distinguish Jennifer from generic tutors. This is the page that converts thoughtful, skeptical parents.

**1. Hero**
- Headline: *"Every child is gifted with something. My job is to find it."*
- Subhead: *"My approach starts with your child's gifts and learning style — then we build academics on that foundation."*

**2. The Four Pillars** (the garden metaphor as content architecture)
- 🌱 **Plant the Seed** — Identifying gifts and learning style
- 🌿 **Tend the Roots** — Closing academic gaps with strategy, not pressure
- 🌳 **Bear Fruit** — Confidence, executive function, self-advocacy
- 🍎 **Sow Forward** — Preparing for the next grade and beyond

Each pillar: short paragraph + illustration + concrete example of what it looks like in practice.

**3. What I Do** (skills covered)
- Reading strategies & comprehension
- Math (number sense, problem-solving, fluency)
- Writing & composition
- Test-taking strategies (FSA / Florida assessments)
- Executive function & organization
- Homework help
- Homeschool curriculum support
- IEP / 504 accommodation strategies

**4. What I Don't Do** (honesty as a trust-builder)
Pulled directly from Jennifer's own materials:
- I'm not a miracle worker — I can't solve effort issues for your child
- I don't guarantee specific score growth (no "from a 1 to a 5" promises)
- I work *with* SLDs and IEPs, but I don't "fix" them — I give your child strategies

**5. The Three Audience Sections** (anchor links from the home page paths)
- **For struggling students** — confidence, test prep, strategy
- **For homeschool families** — partnership, curriculum support, planning
- **For faith-aligned families** — values-aligned education, scripture-informed encouragement

**6. CTA → Contact**

## Build Order (Recommended)

When building in Claude Code, build pages in this order:

1. **Layout component** (header, footer, nav) — used by every page
2. **Home page** — proves the whole design system works end-to-end
3. **Contact page** — the primary CTA, needs to work perfectly
4. **About page** — second-most-visited page
5. **Approach page** — the differentiator
6. **Services page** — pricing requires accuracy
7. **Testimonials page** — simplest, do it last

Each page commits separately. No global refactors mid-build.
