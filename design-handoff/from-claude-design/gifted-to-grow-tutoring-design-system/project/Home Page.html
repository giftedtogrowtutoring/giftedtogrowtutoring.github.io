Meet Jennifer

One teacher. Every session.

When you write to Gifted to Grow, you reach me — not a desk, not a coordinator. I read every note and write back the same day.

I'm Jennifer Pollard. I taught for twenty-five years in Florida public and private K–6 classrooms before opening this tutoring practice. I keep my caseload small so I can know each of your children well — what makes them laugh, what makes them freeze, what kind of week they had.
